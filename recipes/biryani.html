Mutton Biryani

Description

This is one of the most famous recipes in all of Kolkata. It takes approximately 2-4 hours to cook it and is extremely delicious and lovely to taste.

Ingredients

  • Rice
  • Mutton
  • Aloo

Steps:-

  1. Boil the mutton for a couple of hours
  2. Cook the rice and add the ingredients
  3. Cook the aloo and add it.
  4. Cook the mutton and add it.
  5. Mix all the ingredients and cook them together.
