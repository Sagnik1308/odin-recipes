Chicken Fried Rice

Description

An extremely famous Chinese Dish. It is usually eaten in combination with other another Chinese Chicken/Pork/Lamb item.

Ingredients:-

  • Rice
  • Capsicum
  • Carrot
  • Chicken

Steps:-

  1. Cook the rice and add chinese soya sauce and flavours to it.
  2. Add the capsicum and carrot to it.
  3. Cut the chickens in pieces and add it to the rice.
  4. Cook the rice fully and add it and mix it properly.
